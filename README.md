# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Retail Sales Data

## Oasis Infobyte Data Analytics Internship - Task 1

### Project Overview

This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Retail Sales Dataset. The objective is to analyze sales patterns, customer segments, product performance, and regional trends to derive meaningful business insights.

### Objectives

* Load and clean the retail sales dataset.
* Perform descriptive statistical analysis.
* Analyze sales trends over time.
* Explore customer and product performance.
* Visualize data using charts and graphs.
* Generate actionable business recommendations.

### Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Jupyter Notebook

### Dataset Features

The dataset contains:

* Order Date
* Ship Date
* Customer Name
* Segment
* Country
* City
* State
* Region
* Product ID
* Category
* Sub-Category
* Product Name
* Sales

### Steps Performed

#### 1. Data Loading

* Imported the dataset using Pandas.
* Checked dataset structure and dimensions.

#### 2. Data Cleaning

* Checked for missing values.
* Checked duplicate records.
* Converted Order Date column into datetime format.

#### 3. Descriptive Statistics

* Calculated summary statistics.
* Analyzed sales distribution.

#### 4. Time Series Analysis

* Converted sales data into monthly trends.
* Visualized monthly sales performance using line charts.

#### 5. Category Analysis

* Analyzed total sales by category.
* Identified top-performing categories.

#### 6. Regional Analysis

* Compared sales performance across regions and states.

#### 7. Product Analysis

* Identified top-selling products.
* Evaluated product contribution to overall sales.

#### 8. Customer Segment Analysis

* Analyzed sales across customer segments.

### Visualizations Created

* Monthly Sales Trend Line Chart
* Category-wise Sales Bar Chart
* Region-wise Sales Bar Chart
* Top 10 States by Sales
* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Customer Segment Pie Chart

### Key Insights

* Certain product categories contribute significantly to overall sales.
* Sales vary across different regions and states.
* A small group of products generates a large share of revenue.
* Customer segments exhibit different purchasing behaviors.
* Monthly sales trends help identify seasonal demand patterns.

### Recommendations

1. Focus marketing efforts on high-performing categories.
2. Maintain adequate inventory for top-selling products.
3. Expand operations in regions with strong sales performance.
4. Develop targeted campaigns for high-value customer segments.
5. Use historical sales trends for forecasting and planning.
